About Us:

Welcome to the About section of our welfare website. We are dedicated to fostering positive change and supporting communities through compassionate initiatives. Our organization is committed to improving the lives of individuals by providing essential resources and assistance to those facing various challenges. Founded on principles of empathy and solidarity, our mission is to create a more equitable and inclusive society where every person has the opportunity to thrive. We believe in the power of community and collaboration to address societal needs and promote well-being. At Ehsaas, we uphold values of compassion, integrity, and social responsibility. Through our efforts, we strive to make a meaningful impact by advocating for the rights of marginalized groups, promoting sustainable practices, and fostering a culture of empowerment and resilience. Explore our website to learn more about our mission, initiatives, and how you can get involved in making a difference. Together, we can build a brighter future where everyone has the support they need to succeed.

Our Partners:

Edhi Foundation: The Edhi Foundation, founded by Abdul Sattar Edhi, is Pakistan's largest and most respected social welfare organization, providing a wide range of services including healthcare, emergency assistance, and orphanages.

Alkhidmat Foundation: Alkhidmat Foundation is one of the largest charitable organizations in Pakistan, dedicated to humanitarian services such as healthcare, education, orphan care, and community development initiatives.

JDC: JDC is a disaster relief organization in Pakistan that provides emergency assistance, disaster response, and humanitarian aid to those affected by natural disasters and crises.

Chhipa Welfare Association: Chhipa Welfare Association is known for its humanitarian efforts in Pakistan, focusing on providing food, shelter, healthcare, and emergency relief services to the needy and disadvantaged segments of society.
